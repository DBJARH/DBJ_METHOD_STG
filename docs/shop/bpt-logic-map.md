# The Navigation Blueprint

**The BPT Logic Map** is the conceptual blueprint of your good EA Navigaor

### The Three Segments
1.  **B (Business):** The Captain's decision layer. Owners of the investment.
2.  **P (Product):** The Business Analyst layer. Owners of the functional logic.
3.  **T (Technology):** The Implementation layer. Navigating around Legacy Icebergs.

### The Navigator's View
The EA uses this map to ensure that the "Technology Pistons" are capable of delivering the "Business Intent" without hitting hidden legacy risks.
