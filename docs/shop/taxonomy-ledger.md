# DBJ Taxonomy: The Language of the Crew

In the cold of a market storm, miscommunication is a shipwreck. The **DBJ Taxonomy** provides a standardized language base for the whole crew.

### Core Inventory
* **Business Lexicon:** Standardizing terms for ROI, Investability, and Strategic Intent.
* **Product Logic:** Defining the functional requirements used by the Business Analyst (P).
* **Technology Assets:** Precise classification of Legacy systems and AI components.

### Value to the Captain
Eliminates the "Doubt" of interpretation. When a command is issued, the Engine Room (Technology) and the Bridge (Business) share the exact same definition of success.
