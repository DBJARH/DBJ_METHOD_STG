# DBJ ADM Steering Guide: The Steering Wheel

The **DBJ ADM** is the mechanism used to turn the BPT Engine. It translates the Captain’s intent into directional changes.

### Daily Workflow Steps
* **Phase A:** Architecture Vision (The Captain's Goal).
* **Phase B/P:** Business to Product Transition (The BA's Logic).
* **Phase T:** Technology Implementation (Navigating the Legacy Iceberg).

The ADM ensures that no **knee-jerk reaction** goes straight to code without passing through the Navigator's check.
